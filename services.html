How will we serve you?

Product Sourcing

“The identifying and vetting of a product manufacturer”

Nearly every business must buy its products or supplies from a manufacturing source. The question is, where do you buy from? Who is your source? Red Bridge Co. fast tracks your business by seamlessly and quickly connecting you to the best manufacturing source. The effectiveness of our unique sourcing method is proven when you receive your free product quotation. Businesses the world over have been astounded when they see what our sources can do for them.

Production Management

“The oversight and coordination of the manufacturing process”

The typical wholesale order in China usually takes between 60-90 days to complete. During this time, many production decisions and adjustments will need to be made. Red Bridge Co. simplifies this process by understanding your product requirements and then communicating and executing them with the Chinese factory.

Quality Control

“The process used to ensure a production of goods is manufactured according to a specific standard of quality”

Don’t write China off because some have received some bad batches of product. China is the largest manufacturing country in the world for a reason. In fact, more than 20% of global manufacturing output is done by China. How do corporations ensure their products meet their quality standards? Simple. Quality control. The Apple iPhone is made in China. Would you not consider this a quality product? Red Bridge Co. implements specific processes, systems, and inspections to make sure that the only thing that leaves our partner factories is one thing: a quality product.

Translation and Interpretation

“Explaining the meaning of a foreign word in one’s native tongue whether in written form or verbally face-to-face”

It’s not hard to see the differences in culture, customs, and mindset between America and China, especially with regards to the business world. With a decade of hands-on experience and fluency in Mandarin Chinese, Red Bridge Co. erases this huge gap for you. Throughout the production process from A to Z, we will convey and represent your best interests in a world 6,000 miles away, all in the manufacturing country’s mother tongue.
