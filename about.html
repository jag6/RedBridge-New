A Little About Us

Red Bridge Co. is a family-owned and operated business with over 30 years of experience and expertise in buying from mainland China.

Originally based out of California’s beautiful Central Coast, we have recently relocated to Middletown, New York, in the Hudson Valley. Although our new headquarters are on the East Coast, we still mainly operate remotely, serving our clients nationwide. We thoroughly enjoy connecting with new clients and guiding them through the sourcing process.
